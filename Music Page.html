Purple Rain

Purple Rain was a 1984 American rock musical drama film directed by Albert Magnoli and written by Magnoli and William Blinn, in which Prince made his film debut.. Purple Rain the sold over 13 million copies and spent 24 consecutive weeks at No .1 on the Billoard 200 charts.

Batman

1989 the Batman Sound track was released .The song Batdance reached the number 1 Spot and this was Prince’s fourth number one single .

The Best of Prince

The very best of Prince is the greatest hits album by the recording artist Prince it was released in 2001 by Warner Brother it contains all of Princes successful singles from 1979 – 1991 including the US number 1s "When Doves Cry", "Let's Go Crazy", "Kiss" and "Cream
