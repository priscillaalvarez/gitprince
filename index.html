Prince Rogers Nelson (June 7,1958 – April 21, 2016) born on the 7th June 1958 in Minneapolis the son of Mattie Della and John Lewis Nelson. Prince's Father was a pianist and songwriter, so this wear Prince developed his skills as a singer, songwriter, instrumentalist, record producer and actor for over 3 decades. Prince was able to play over 30 instruments and also won 7 Grammy Awards a Golden Globe Award and Academy Award for his film Purple Rain. Purple Rain was a 1984 American rock musical drama film directed by Albert Magnoli and written by Magnoli and William Blinn, in which Prince made his film debut..
